Detailed Description

SLKDTree implements the kd-tree space partitioning structure.

A kd-tree is an axis aligned binary space partitioning (BSP) structure that recursively splits the space on one dimension x, y or z. The art is the fast finding of the optimal split position in a way that produce big empty cells.
